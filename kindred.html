A New Type of Friendship App

The Goal:

Design an app that will help post-college adults find and develop friendships with like-minded individuals who share the same interests and values.

The Project:

Understand the stressors and pain points that prevent adults from making new friends.

Problem Statement:

A millennial entrepreneur, wants to make new friends but, because she is new to her city, feels out of her element trying to meet people outside of the workplace. She places importance on seeking like-minded people and wants to make sure they live close in proximity.
How might we provide a mobile application for adults seeking new friendships that will allow them to find others based on proximity, availability, and shared interests and values?
